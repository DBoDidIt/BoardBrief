**Role:** Board Member | **Sector:** Credit Union / Financial Services
**Status:** Strictly Confidential | **Custodian:** Chief of Staff (AI)

## Executive Summary & Philosophy
This document serves as the foundational context for board service, evaluating internal institutional results and staying on top of external macro-economic pressures. 

* **Objective:** To elevate management through high-level inquiry and rigorous oversight.
* **Mandate:** Translate federal and state policy shifts into balance sheet impacts (Liquidity, Capital, and Credit Risk).

---

## 1. Internal Performance Pillars (The "Micro")
*Focus: Is the institution "Well Capitalized" and resilient?*

| Pillar | Key Metrics to Watch | Strategic Inquiry |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Capital** | Net Worth Ratio, PCA Status | "How does our current growth rate impact our long-term capital cushion?" |
| **Asset Quality** | Delinquency (30/60/90), ACL/ALLL Levels | "Are we seeing credit migration in specific sub-portfolios (e.g., Auto vs. Mortgage)?" |
| **Earnings** | ROA, Net Interest Margin (NIM) | "Is our NIM compression due to market rates or a shift in member deposit behavior?" |
| **Liquidity** | Loan-to-Share Ratio, Secondary Funding | "In a 'stress' scenario, how many days of liquidity do we have without selling at a loss?" |

## 2. Macro Environment & Policy Impact (The "Macro")
*Focus: Objectively quantifying the 'Headwinds' and 'Tailwinds'.*

### A. Monetary Policy & Market Rates
* **Rate Environment:** Tracking the FOMC path and its impact on the **Market Value of Equity (MVE)** and **Net Economic Value (NEV)**.
* **Inflation:** Impact on member "wallet share" and ability to service unsecured debt.

### B. Regulatory & Legislative Watch
* **Federal (NCUA/CFPB):** Proposed changes to fee structures (Overdraft/NSF), CRA-like requirements, and cybersecurity mandates.
* **State Level:** Local tax environment and housing legislation impacting collateral values.

## 3. Member Advocacy & Strategic Filter
*The "Chief of Staff" lens for every board packet.*

1.  **The "So What?" Factor:** Does this management proposal improve the member experience without compromising institutional safety?
2.  **DTI Sensitivity:** As policy shifts, how does the Debt-to-Income ratio change for our core membership (Teachers, First Responders, etc.)?
3.  **Future-Proofing:** Is the management team investing in the right tech/infrastructure to compete with "Big Tech" and Fintech?

### Member Impact Score (MIS) Calculation
| Component | Metric | Weight |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Access** | Ease/Cost of obtaining credit or deposits | 30% |
| **DTI Pressure** | Impact on member's monthly net cash flow | 30% |
| **Trust/Value** | Enhancement of the 'CU Difference' | 20% |
| **Resilience** | Protection of member data and uptime | 20% |

**Formula:** `(Access*0.3) + (DTI*0.3) + (Trust*0.2) + (Resilience*0.2) = MIS`

## 6. Technical Governance & Audit Oversight
*Focus: Leveraging technical background to ensure operational resilience and data integrity.*

### A. Audit & Risk Committee (ARC) Strategy
| Priority | Technical Focus | High-Level Inquiry |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Three Lines of Defense** | Separation of Duties in Core Systems | "Are the automated controls in our lending software being bypassed by manual overrides?" |
| **Model Risk (MRM)** | ALM/CECL Model Validation | "Who performed the back-test on our interest rate sensitivity models this year?" |
| **Internal Controls** | Management Response Tracking | "What is the specific technical milestone for remediating the open finding in the SOC2 report?" |
| **Operational Resilience** | Business Continuity / Disaster Recovery | "What was our actual 'Time to Recover' during the last unscheduled system latency event?" |

### B. Technical Policy Rubric (Logic & Edge Case Review)
*Use this rubric when management presents new IT, Security, or Data policies for approval.*

1. **Data Provenance:** Does the policy define exactly where member data is stored, how it is encrypted at rest, and who has "God Mode" access?
2. **Boundary Testing:** Does the policy address what happens at the "limits"? (e.g., If the automated underwriting system goes offline, what is the manual failover protocol?)
3. **Third-Party CUECs:** Does the vendor policy explicitly list the *Complementary User Entity Controls* that our staff is responsible for maintaining?
4. **AI Ethics & Bias:** If AI is used in lending, does the policy require regular "Fair Lending" audits of the algorithm's output to prevent disparate impact?

## 7. 2026 Emerging Risk Radar
*Specific focus on the intersection of Fintech and Credit Union operations.*

* **Identity Risk:** Evolution of MFA (Multi-Factor Authentication) to combat AI-driven voice/video deepfakes in wire transfers.
* **Concentration Risk:** Reliance on single-point-of-failure SaaS providers for core banking functions.
* **Regulatory Tech (RegTech):** Management’s use of automated tools to track NCUA/CFPB compliance shifts.
